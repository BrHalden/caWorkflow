Routes

Routes in and around Bergen city. Find routes for every occation: site seeing tour, training, Off-road, Mountain, Urban

We have gathered a selection of routes that can take you to the most important locations in and around Bergen. We focus on environment friendly and effective travel. The Urban routes will take you through the cycling roads in Bergen, while the mountain routs will take you into various mountain tracks located near Bergen.

About Us

We focus on cycling routes in Bergen, and try to make everyones biking experince better and more eco friendly.

Our focus is mainly directed towards guiding tourists through Bergen in a eco friendly manner. We have been adding other categories, but mainly we focus on guiding people from one location to the next, with quick and accessible websoulitions from both phone and computer, without driving cars or busses around.
